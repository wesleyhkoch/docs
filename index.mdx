## Onboarding Flow

Follow these four steps to go from zero to your first successful API call.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an Integration Access">
    Log in to the GoMoney platform and create a new **INTEGRATION** access for your project. This registers your application and unlocks API-level permissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request Your Credentials">
    Contact GoMoney support to receive your `clientKey`, `clientSecret`, and allowed `domain`. Keep these values secret — you will use them in every authenticated request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authenticate">
    Call the login endpoint to obtain a **Bearer JWT token**. Then Base64-encode your `clientKey:clientSecret` pair to build your **secret-key** header. Both values are required for protected routes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register Customers and Add Balances">
    Use the Customers endpoints to register your end-users, then trigger PIX-based deposits to add GMC token balance to their accounts.
  </Step>
</Steps>
